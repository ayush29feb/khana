Catalog

Snap a label. It's in your catalog forever.

Send Claude a photo of any nutrition label. It reads the macros, confirms every number with you, and adds the food to your catalog. Every food you've ever bought lives here — once it's in, you never enter its macros again.

📷

Works with any label. Serving size, protein, carbs, fat, calories — Claude reads them all and waits for your confirmation before saving anything.

Pantry

A live inventory of everything in your kitchen.

Every time you buy groceries, Khana adds to your pantry. Every time you cook a home meal, it deducts the servings used. At any point you can ask exactly what's left — and how much total protein that represents.

Meals

Log what you ate. Macros calculated automatically.

For home meals, tell Claude the ingredients and servings — it maps them to your catalog, calculates the macros, and deducts from your pantry. For restaurant meals, just give the numbers. Estimates are always fine.

Goals

Set a macro target. See your daily pace.

Set a goal for any date range — a cut, a bulk, a maintenance phase. The Home page shows how much of your daily target you've hit, whether you're on pace, and a breakdown by day. Multiple goals for different phases.

new goal — "April Cut", April 1–30. 160g protein, 200g carbs, 60g fat, 2200 cal per day
April Cut  ·  Apr 1 – Apr 30
160g P  ·  200g C  ·  60g F  ·  2,200 kcal / day

Trends

See how you've been doing over time.

The Trends page shows your cumulative progress against each goal over the selected date range. See whether you're hitting your targets week over week — without having to ask Claude every time.

How it works

Your phone. Your voice. Your data.

Khana runs on your Mac and you interact with it from your phone via Claude Code — no app to install, no account to create.

1

Open Claude on your phone

Connect to Claude Code running on your Mac via remote access. Claude has full context about your catalog, pantry, and goals.

2

Just talk

Tell Claude what you ate, share a photo of a nutrition label, or ask how you're tracking against your goals. Claude handles the rest.

3

View your data on the dashboard

Open the web dashboard on your phone browser via Tailscale. See meals, trends, pantry inventory, and goal progress — all visualized.

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for daily use — fast to log, easy to review, yours to own.

📷

Photo label reading

Share a photo of any nutrition label. Claude reads the macros and confirms before adding.

🥫

Pantry tracking

Log groceries and Khana tracks what you have. Home meals automatically deduct from inventory.

🎯

Goal pacing

Set macro targets for a date range. See daily progress and whether you're on pace.

📊

Trends

Daily macro breakdown charts over any date range. Spot patterns in your eating habits.

🔒

Fully local

SQLite database on your Mac. No cloud, no subscription, no data leaving your machine.

📱

Mobile-first

Dashboard accessible on your phone via Tailscale. Log from anywhere with the Claude app.

Stack

Simple and open.

Three components, one SQLite file, no external services required.

⌘

CLI — Python + Typer

All data entry. Runs locally, driven by Claude Code. The khana command handles catalog, pantry, meals, and goals.

⬡

Server — Node.js + GraphQL

Reads the SQLite database, serves the dashboard API, and handles image uploads and static file serving.

◈

Dashboard — React + Relay + Vite

Data visualization. Accessible on your phone via Tailscale. Read-only — all writes go through Claude.
